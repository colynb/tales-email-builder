Issue #23 - Oct 24, 2014

By Colyn Brown under issues

Greetings! Welcome to issue #23. It's the annual Tales pledge drive... wait put your wallets down! You don't have to give me loads of cash like you always do! If you get any value from the effort I put into this every week, show me some love - with a re-tweet! Every day I scour the internet for the most interesting content with regard to all things frontend. Then each week, I take the best from that list and present them here in this newsletter. If you like what you're reading and get some value out of my efforts, you can help me out by telling your friends about it and follow me on twitter, and give me a re-tweet every now and then. Other than hard cash, it's the only way to measure whether or not I'm providing value. You can find me at my new twitter account, @frontendd. Thanks!
